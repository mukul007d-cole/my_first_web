Pushkar Fair

The Pushkar Fair is an annual eight-day fair that takes place in the city of Pushkar in the Ajmer district of Rajasthan. The fair is also known as the Pushkar Camel Fair, Kartik Mela, or Kartik ka Mela. The fair is one of the largest cattle fairs in the world

  • Date -
  • Mon, 20 Nov, 2023 - Tue, 28 Nov, 2023
Open

Pushkar International Holi

Holi in Pushkar is celebrated at the famous chowk where thousands of people gather together throwing gulaal in the air, some high on bhaang, and others, on life. It is well known throughout the World.

  • Date -
  • March 8, 2023

Urs of KhwajaMoinddin Chisti

This festival is celebrated with full fervor in the May month of every year. It is used to conduct at the Dargah of the Sufi Saint and active participation of all the people of Ajmer makes it more colorful and enjoyable.
